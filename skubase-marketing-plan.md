# skubase Marketing Plan

## Positioning in One Line
The Shopify inventory tool that tells you what to do today — ranked actions, not spreadsheets.

## Why This Moment
Stocky shut down. Thousands of Shopify merchants are actively looking for a replacement right now. That window closes as the market settles on alternatives. The next 60–90 days are the highest-leverage time to acquire early customers who will become long-term advocates.

---

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

**Primary:** Shopify merchant doing $500K–$5M annual revenue, 200–5,000 active SKUs, currently using Stocky, a spreadsheet, or nothing structured.

**Secondary:** Growing Shopify brand that outgrew spreadsheets but isn't ready for an enterprise WMS.

**Pain points:**
- Stockouts on top SKUs, overstock on slow movers
- No clear signal on what to reorder first
- Vendor lead times tracked in spreadsheets
- Dead stock eating working capital
- No forecasting — ordering by gut feel

**What they've tried:** Stocky, spreadsheets, Inventory Planner, Cin7, Skubana/Extensiv (too expensive/complex)

**What they want:** Something that works like Shopify — simple, visual, tells me what to do.

---

## Differentiation

| Competitor | Problem | Our answer |
|---|---|---|
| Stocky | Shut down | We're the direct replacement |
| Spreadsheets | Manual, no forecasting, breaks at scale | Automated, ranked queue, forecasting built in |
| Inventory Planner | Expensive, steep learning curve | 14-day trial, self-serve, published prices |
| Cin7 / Skubana | Enterprise overkill, $1K+/mo | $49–$349/mo, built for Shopify-first merchants |

**Unique angle:** Independent, founder-led, price-lock pledge. No PE, no surprise renewal hikes. Prices published on the site.

## Channel Strategy

### 1. Reddit (Immediate — Week 1–2)

**Stocky migration threads (highest intent)**
- r/shopify, r/stocky, r/ecommerce
- Find threads: "Stocky alternative", "what are you switching to", "inventory management recommendations"
- Approach: Answer genuinely, mention skubase at end. Don't lead with the pitch.

**Template comment:**
> We switched off Stocky too and couldn't find anything that fit so we built skubase.io — Shopify OAuth, pulls your full catalog and 12 months of orders, gives you a ranked action queue (reorder / optimize / liquidate) instead of a table you have to figure out yourself. 14-day trial no card. Happy to answer questions.

**Create your own thread (1 per week max):**
- "What are Shopify merchants using now that Stocky is gone?" — share your story, mention skubase as what you built
- "How we think about inventory for Shopify brands doing $1M–$5M" — value-first, product at end

---

### 2. Facebook Groups (Week 1–4)

High-value groups:
- Shopify Entrepreneurs (800K+ members)
- eCommerce Fuel (vetted, high quality)
- Shopify Dropshipping & Store Owners
- Klaviyo / Shopify App Users groups

Approach: Search for posts mentioning "inventory", "Stocky", "out of stock", "reorder". Comment with value. DM anyone who responds to the Stocky threads with a direct offer: free 30-day trial, help with onboarding.

---

### 3. Shopify App Store (Week 2–4)

**This is your most important long-term channel.**

Steps:
1. Complete the app submission ("Manage submission" in Shopify Partners)
2. Write the listing copy (see App Store section below)
3. Get 5–10 early users to leave reviews before broader launch
4. Target keywords: "inventory management", "reorder point", "Stocky replacement", "demand forecasting"

The App Store creates passive inbound. Every merchant who searches for Stocky alternatives or inventory tools will find you without any spend.

---

### 4. Direct Outreach (Week 1–8)

**Stocky users:**
- Post in any Stocky community: "If you're looking for a replacement, DM me — I'll extend your trial to 30 days and personally help you get set up."
- Find LinkedIn posts about Stocky shutdown — comment and connect

**Shopify agency partners:**
- Email 20–30 Shopify Plus agencies and development shops
- Offer: white-label referral deal or partner commission (20% MRR for 12 months)
- They onboard new merchants constantly and need tool recommendations

**Inventory-heavy niches:** Apparel, supplements, beauty, home goods — high SKU count, high reorder complexity. Find Facebook groups or Slack communities specific to these niches.

Each article should end with a clear CTA to start a free trial.

### 6. Email Nurture (Week 2+)

Every trial signup gets a 5-email sequence:

**Day 0 — Welcome:** "Here's the one thing to do in your first 10 minutes" (connect Shopify store or import Stocky CSV)

**Day 2 — First value:** "Your action queue is ready — here's what the numbers mean" (explain urgent/optimize/dead categories)

**Day 5 — Feature spotlight:** "Are you using the forecast?" — explain Holt forecasting, why gut feel overstocks you

**Day 10 — Social proof / urgency:** "3 things merchants find in their first week" + reminder trial ends in 4 days

**Day 13 — Last chance:** Plain text from Rainer — "Trial ends tomorrow. Anything I can help with before you decide?"

---

### 7. ProductHunt (Week 4–6)

Time a PH launch for when you have 10+ real users who can upvote and leave reviews on launch day.

**Preparation:**
- Get a hunter with an audience (ask in PH Slack or Maker communities)
- Line up 15–20 people to upvote on launch day (customers, friends, LinkedIn)
- Write a strong tagline: "The Shopify inventory tool that tells you what to reorder today"
- Prepare a short demo GIF or video

**Timing:** Tuesday–Thursday launch, 12:01am PT (when PH resets)

---

### 8. Shopify App Store Listing Copy

**Tagline:** Ranked inventory actions for Shopify — reorder, optimize, and liquidate in one queue.

**Description (first 170 chars — what shows in search):**
skubase turns your Shopify catalog into a daily action queue. See exactly what to reorder, what to optimize, and what dead stock is costing you — no spreadsheets.

**Key features to highlight:**
- One-click Shopify OAuth — live inventory and 12 months of orders in minutes
- Action queue ranked by urgency, not just sorted by name
- Holt demand forecasting with seasonality
- Supplier scorecards — on-time rate, fill rate, lead time stability
- Dead-stock liquidation plans with dollar-impact estimates
- Stocky CSV import for easy migration
