# LinkedIn outreach — G2/Capterra negative-review writers

## How to use

1. On G2.com, search "Inventory Planner" / "Linnworks" / "Cin7 Core" / "SKUVault" / "Skubana".
2. Filter to 1- and 2-star reviews from the last 12 months.
3. Each reviewer leaves their company name. Find them on LinkedIn.
4. Send the DM below. **Personalize the first line** with one specific detail from their review.
5. Target: 10/day for 30 days. Track responses in a spreadsheet.

Conversion on this is shockingly high — these people are pre-qualified buyers in an active switching mood. Don't sell. Just open the door.

## Anti-patterns

- **Don't send the same template to 100 people.** Personalize one line every time. LinkedIn flags template farms.
- **Don't ask for a meeting in the first message.** Just open the door.
- **Don't follow up more than once.** If they don't reply, they're not interested. Move on.
- **Don't pretend to be neutral.** Disclose that you're the founder of slelfly.
